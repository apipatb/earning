## üí° QUICK WINS (Do This Week)

1. **Undercut Initially**
   - First 5 VA clients: $5 below market rate
   - First 10 dog walks: 20% discount
   - Result: Fast reviews, then raise rates

2. **Multi-App Delivery**
   - Run DoorDash + Uber Eats simultaneously
   - Accept best orders from either
   - Result: 30-50% more earnings per hour

3. **Bundle Services**
   - "Weekly dog walking: 5 walks for $80" (vs. $20 each)
   - "4 articles/month for $300" (vs. $100 each)
   - Result: Recurring income, client convenience

4. **Leverage Dead Time**
   - Between deliveries: Quick surveys
   - Waiting for clients: Apply to jobs
   - Result: Additional $50-100/week

5. **Ask for Referrals**
   - After every 5-star review: "Know anyone else who needs this?"
   - Offer referral bonus: "$10 off for you and your friend"
   - Result: Client acquisition cost = $0
