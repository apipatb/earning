## Marketing Strategy

### Target Audience

**Primary:**
- Freelancers (designers, developers, writers)
- Gig workers (Uber, DoorDash, delivery)
- Multi-platform earners
- Content creators (YouTube, TikTok, OnlyFans)

**Demographics:**
- Age: 25-45
- Location: Global (US, Europe, Asia)
- Income: $2K-10K/month
- Tech-savvy

**Pain Points:**
- Hard to track income across multiple platforms
- Don't know actual hourly rate
- No clear goals or progress tracking
- Manual spreadsheets are tedious
- Tax time is nightmare
