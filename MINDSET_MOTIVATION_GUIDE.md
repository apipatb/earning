## ðŸ§˜ MENTAL HEALTH & SUSTAINABILITY

### Signs You're Burning Out

**Physical:**
- Constant fatigue (even after sleep)
- Headaches, body aches
- Getting sick frequently
- Insomnia or sleeping too much

**Emotional:**
- Irritability, short temper
- Feeling numb or apathetic
- Crying easily
- Dreading work

**Mental:**
- Can't focus
- Forgetting things
- Making mistakes
- Negative self-talk increasing

**Behavioral:**
- Isolating from friends/family
- Neglecting self-care
- Using alcohol/food to cope
- Procrastinating more than usual

**If you check 5+ boxes, you need a break. Now.**

### Burnout Prevention

**1. Non-Negotiable Rest:**
- 1 full day off per week (no work, no checking apps)
- 7-8 hours sleep (not optional)
- 30 min outside daily (walk, sunlight)

**2. Boundaries:**
- Work hours end at set time
- No client messages after 9 PM
- Weekend work = weekend only, not 24/7

**3. Energy Management:**
- High-energy hours = high-value work
- Low-energy hours = low-demand work
- No-energy hours = rest (not push through)

**4. Joy Injection:**
- One thing daily just for fun
- Hobby, friends, creativity
- Work can't be your whole identity

**5. Support System:**
- Talk to someone weekly
- Join online community
- Accountability partner
- Therapist if needed (not weakness)

### When to Seek Professional Help

**See a therapist if:**
- Burnout symptoms persist despite rest
- Anxiety interfering with work/life
- Depression lasting more than 2 weeks
- Using substances to cope
- Thoughts of self-harm

**Resources:**
- BetterHelp, Talkspace (online, $60-90/week)
- OpenPath Collective ($30-80/session)
- Local sliding scale clinics
- Employee Assistance Programs (if applicable)

**Your mental health is the foundation of your earning ability. Protect it.**

### Focus Framework

**The 80/20 Rule:**
80% of results come from 20% of activities.

**Your job:**

2. Do more of that
3. Do less of everything else

**Example After Month 1:**

- Surveys: $10/hr (10 hours/week) = $100
